What is SeedStack ?

SeedStack is an enterprise-class development stack aimed at providing best-in-class solutions for common software development needs. It is built around three main frameworks: the Java framework, the Web framework, the Business framework, built on top of the two others, providing high-level abstractions and building blocks for realizing your business needs.

Manifesto

SeedStack is a global solution for developing modern applications. Here are our main goals.

Offer a vision


Through opinionated choices and concrete building blocks to back them.

Accelerate the devs


By providing a focused and powerful technical base which will save you days of coding.

Reduce the cost


By helping develop durable and maintainable software.

Facilitate reuse


Via great modularity and a common base to share your realization with others.

Encourage experimentation


By allowing you to quickly implement your next idea in a way that can scale if needed.

Modernize your software


Through the usage of current technologies and best-practices.

Improve the dev experience


Through a solution that just works out-of-the-box and won't get in your way.

Share the code


To allow every developer to understand the SeedStack inner workings and eventually be a part of it.
