What is SeedStack ?

SeedStack is an enterprise-class development stack aimed at providing best-in-class solutions for common software development needs. It is built around three main frameworks: the Java framework, the Web framework, the Business framework, built on top of the two others, providing high-level abstractions and building blocks for realizing your business needs.

  • Easy to use,
  • Opinionated,
  • Technically advanced,
  • Ready to use.

Manifesto

SeedStack is a global solution for developing modern applications. Here are our main goals.

Offer a vision


Through opinionated choices and concrete building blocks to back it.

Reduce the cost


By deploying a common, yet modular, solution to a majority of projects.

Accelerate the devs


By providing a simple-to-use and powerful technical base which will save you from coding the same thing again and again.

Facilitate reuse


Via great modularity and a common base to share your realization with others.

Encourage experimentation


By minimizing the time-to-run and enabling prototypes to become full-blown applications in a very short time.

Modernize the software


Through the usage of current technologies and best-practices and by paying attention to what's next.

Improve the dev experience


Through a solution that just works out-of-the-box and won't get in the way of your needs and requirements.

Share the code


To allow every developer to understand the SeedStack inner workings and be a part of it.
